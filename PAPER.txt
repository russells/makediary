* Paper size notes

Look in makediary/PaperSize.py for a list of supported paper sizes.

** B5

500    x 710    points
176.37 x 250.44 mm

Ricoh printer does not have a size for B5.

gv calls this ISOB5, but it does not appear in the selectable list of
sizes.

** B5J

517    x 730    points
182.36 x 257.50 mm

Ricoh printer calls this B5-JIS.

gv calls this B5, and it does appear in the list.
